Food For All DC

Fall 2024

Established in 1984, Food for All DC delivers pre-packaged groceries to homebound and non-homebound food insecure persons in the DC area. Their mission is to provide groceries with care and dignity.

  • Serving over 1,000 families monthly
  • Partnering with 20+ local organizations
  • Driven by a dedicated team of volunteers
